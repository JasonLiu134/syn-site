How to Play

You choose where Pax goes:

As the player, you get to nudge Pax to look for inspiration from a range of locations; Pax can:

  • look around at Terrestrial species,
  • below the water for Marine species,
  • up above for Flying species,
  • and/or down below for Plant/Fungi species.

Where Pax looks will define what kinds of species it is able to observe, and thus what kind of alternative experiences it might learn from.

Pax learns on its own:

Once it looks in a place, it will focus on a certain Species and an Individual within that species.

Which species and individual Pax finds interesting is not up to you!

Pax decides what to do:

What Pax learns from each species and individual will help it decide what to do next, with each giving points that contribute to one of three different endings of the game.

In particular, each Species card will give 1 point towards an ending, and each Individual card will give 0.5 points to its respective ending.

Once Pax has accrued enough points towards one of the endings, then it will decide what to do, both with itself, the world, and what remains of humanity...

You can view which species Pax has observed and the progress it has made to each ending in the History tab.

Four Game Modes:

1. Normal Mode: The default game mode that uses the elements discussed above.

2. Quick Mode: Similar to normal mode, but reaches an ending faster.

3. Selective Mode: You are also able to select specific species in addition to location.

4. Random Mode: Location and species are randomly assigned automatically.
